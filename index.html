Returning to Haifa is a space dedicated to telling the truth about Palestine as a colonized land and a people fighting erasure. This site documents the history of Palestine before Zionist settlement, the Nakba as a planned campaign of ethnic cleansing, and the ongoing reality of apartheid, siege, and displacement.

We stand with Palestinian resistance as a legitimate struggle against colonial domination and refuse the language that hides these crimes behind words like “conflict” or “clashes.” Our goal is to give people clear facts, accessible history, and tools to recognize propaganda – so that Palestine cannot be erased, on the ground or in memory.
